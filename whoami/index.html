About this blog

the what

This is my garage with the door open. I decided to revive the blog to share how my on-going projects are doing (and provide some motivation for myself to finish them), share some interesting links I stumbled upon, lay down my thoughts in written form or just post some dirty hack that helped me and might help someone else. While I plan to keep this thing more or less technical the subtitle is basically accurate: it is my omnium gatherum, not some topic-specific publication, so one can expect all range of content. I try to keep it SFW, except of odd (or even) swearword.

the how

The blog is powered by hugo static generator and currently hosted via github pages.

I do not collect any of your data, I have no idea what to do with it. (though embedded content might track you, consider an appropriate browser extension and a donation to EFF’esque foundation in your area if this is a concern).

This site supposed to work with js disabled (except of an odd video embed). If something is not degrading gracefully please let me know.

the caveat

Though I dare to say that I speak english fluently I still would occasionally put an extra comma somewhere where it doesn’t really belong or forget to feed the post to the spellchecker before pushing blog online :).
